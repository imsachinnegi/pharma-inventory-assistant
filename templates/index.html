💊 Pharma Inventory Assistant

📋 Available Commands:

  • show inventory - View all medicines
  • low stock - Show medicines with quantity < 10
  • expiring soon - Show medicines expiring in 30 days
  • search [medicine name] - Search for a medicine
  • add medicine - Add new medicine (provide details)
  • sell [quantity] [medicine] - Reduce stock
  • delete [medicine name] - Delete a medicine
